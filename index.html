Who We Are

Passionate about serving our community.

DOTWORLD was established in 2017 by 4 rural youngsters located in Eastern Cape, Our goal was to provide a creative, innovative, vibrant environment to network and have a cup of coffee at the same time. Along our timeline we want to Create Job, Learning opportunities and develop talent of the youth and youngsters of Umtata.

Create Opportunities

Depending on what is handy, we want to introduce growth and knowledge opportunities to our customers, this may includes assistance with research Projects, Assignments, creating CVs, etc.

Define Talent

This process is designed to attract, develop,motivate,and retain productive ,engaged young minds.We want to create sustainable opportunities that meets its strategic and operational goals and objectives in a digital environment.

What We Do

We have everything you need to stay in sync with the digital environment.

We will offer the community easy and affordable access to internet. DotWord will fully provide access to various intranet applications such as, WWW, FTP, email etc. Will also provide unique printing of T-shirts and cultural cloth designs, moreover provide coffee environment with its freshly baked bakery items to our customers.

PC Support

This includes Laptops, Tablets, Desktops, Workstations, Monitors and Docks. We provide software updates, Software installations virus scans. We install and update antiviruses to keep your machines virus and trojan free.

Networking

From peer to peer Networking to social media. We assist with creating proffessional networks e.g LinkedIn, offerzen accounts, We will help you connect with the right people, on the right platforms, the goal is to connect job seekers to their potential jobs.

Web Designs

This includes Web Designs, Birthday cards, Wedding cards, Brochure designs, Graphic Designs,Business cards, Company Logos. The goal is to equip youngsters with productful skills and provide them with new job opportunities.

Cloth Designs

This includes cultural Cloth designs, T-shirts and Sweater printings.There will be coffe and a mini backery The goal for this route is get the youth off the street while having them earn and provide for their families.

Internet Access

The ability of individuals and organizations to connect to the Internet using computer terminals, computers, and other devices e.g Laptops & Mobile devices; and to access services such as email,vacancy hubs,like LinkedIn and the World Wide Web.

Personal Development

Our members will be submitted to a 4 months online course, also serving as a Skills Development course, Our Workstations can be used to access other courses of a members best interest and learning interests.
